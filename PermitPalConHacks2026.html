Our Coverage

5 Ontario Jurisdictions Indexed

Full bylaw coverage across the Waterloo Region and Greater Toronto Area.

Waterloo, ON

A tech-forward municipality with strong support for innovation-driven businesses. Bylaw 2018-050 governs home occupation and commercial zoning.

Base Business Licence: $285/year
Audit Frequency: Annual
Heritage Zones: Uptown Core
Home Occupation Limit: 25% GFA
Processing Time: 4–8 weeks
Lic. Base: $285/yr

Kitchener, ON

A major economic hub with flexible live-work zoning in D-1 districts. High-traffic commercial operations require dedicated occupancy certificates.

Base Business Licence: $320/year
Audit Frequency: Bi-Annual
Live-Work Zones: D-1 District
Governing Bylaw: 85-1
Processing Time: 6–10 weeks
Lic. Base: $320/yr

Cambridge, ON

Heritage Conservation Districts (HCDs) apply to the Galt and Preston cores. Industrial startups must demonstrate fire route accessibility compliance.

Base Business Licence: $260/year
Audit Frequency: Annual
Heritage Districts: Galt + Preston HCDs
Industrial Constraint: Fire Route Access
Processing Time: 3–6 weeks
Lic. Base: $260/yr

Toronto, ON (GTA)

Canada's most complex municipal regulatory environment. Chapter 545 of the Toronto Municipal Code sets the highest bar for PSS and childcare operations.

Base Business Licence: $495/year
Audit Frequency: Quarterly
Commercial Tax Multiplier: 3.8x Residential
Governing Code: Chapter 545
Processing Time: 10–16 weeks
Lic. Base: $495/yr

Guelph, ON

A growing city with strong environmental bylaws. Green building standards apply to new commercial construction. Food service has strong public health unit presence.

Base Business Licence: $295/year
Audit Frequency: Annual
Green Standards: LEED Encouraged
Food Permit: Wellington-Dufferin-Guelph PHU
Processing Time: 4–7 weeks
Lic. Base: $295/yr
